About

Rocko's Dog Food is a monthy subscription service that delivers fresh, local food straight to your house so you can feed the love of your life - your dog. Our smart calculators and flexible plans let you keep your dog healthy and happy.

We also look after dogs in need. For every pack you buy, dog food will be donated to organisations and individuals who donate their time and money to looking after dogs. They really apprecaite you and Rocko's support.
